Walter Farley was an american author who focused mostly on horse stories.
He wrote the Black stallion while both in high school and undergrad. He and his wife had four children; whom they raised on a farm and also at their beach house.

He was born in NY where a lot of the first book is set, and he died in FL. He was even honored by a library in his home town before he died.
