The Black Stallion

Walter Farley

This novel tells the story of a young boy named Alec and a wild
stallion. They meet after the ship they were on wrecks, and as the only
survivers they rely on each other why they await rescue.

Eventually they are picked up by a fishing vessel and taken back to the
the States. The stallion escapes from his home to a farm outside of town.
Alec spends all day and night following clues to find him; he runs into the
owner who comments on the wildness of the horse and says he needs to be
trained. The boy talks him into helping him, and they come to discover how fast
fast the horse his. The train him as a racehorse but because he has no papers
the only way he could compete would be a match-race. The ending of the story
has Alec and the Black in a race agaist two of the fastest horses in the
the States.

They obviously win, as it is a kids story. The movie especially
has great cinematorgraphy of their time on the island; it stars Mickey Rooney
Kelly Reno. Having horses myself, I was obessed with all things equine.
