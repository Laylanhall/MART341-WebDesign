Easy Veggie Enchilada's for College Students

Ingredients

Enchilada Filling:

  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can pinto beans
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 fresh tomato
  • 1 cup sliced and quartered zucchini
  • 1 1/2 cups taco cheese mix
  • fills up to 12 burrito size tortillas

External Ingredients:

  • 2 cans Old El Paso RED Enchilada Sauce
  • 2 cups taco cheese mix
  • 13x9-inch pan
  • Cooking spray or butter for pan grease

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375o F.

Enchilada Filling

  1. In a large bowl, combine chopped onion, quartered zucchini, and tomato.
  2. Rinse both cans of beans in a colander before adding them to the bowl with the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Finally, add cheese to the bowl and mix together.
  4. Grease pan
  5. Grab a tortilla and add a scoop of filling, fold edges together and role tortilla into burrito. Place in pan. Repeat until filled.
  6. Once pan is full, pour sauce over top until santurated.
  7. Cover enchiladas with cheese.
  8. Place on middle oven rack.
  9. Bake for 20-25 min.
  10. Take out and let sit for 5-10 min
