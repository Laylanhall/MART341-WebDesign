Hey there!

My name is Layla, and I LOVE the outdoors. Like, sickly obsessed. I was born in the Flathead Valley, though I spent most childhood in Texas, Colorado, and WI. During high school, I decided I was sick of missing the mountains and immediatly moved Home after graduation. I attended FVCC for awhile and am working to complete my BA in Psychology from the University of Montana. I have been a fulltime resident for the past six years, it has been the only place to feel like home.
